In Memoriam

Sensei Miguel Vélez

Sensei Miguel Vélez, though he passed before the founding of the Kenpo International Federation (KIF), remains a foundational figure in its spirit and legacy.

“You were not just a friend, you were my master.”

Beyond martial arts, Sensei Miguel Vélez was known as a devoted son, husband, father, and friend—an exceptional sensei whose impact continues to be felt by all who had the privilege of knowing him.

Sensei Rigoberto Samayoa

Sensei Rigoberto Samayoa is honored as the second inductee into the Kenpo International Federation (KIF) Hall of Fame.

Sensei Mohamad Tabatabai

Sensei Mohamad Tabatabai is honored as a distinguished inductee into the Kenpo International Federation (KIF) Hall of Fame.
