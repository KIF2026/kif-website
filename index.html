About KIF

Mission & Vision

In the world of American Kenpo Karate, each practitioner is unique, bringing different skills, experiences, and perspectives. Under the leadership of Master Jorge Mario Calvillo, KIF exists to unite practitioners and schools under one guiding principle: to preserve, protect, and transmit traditional American Kenpo Karate as originally developed by Grandmaster Ed Parker.

Preserve, protect, and transmit traditional American Kenpo.
Unite practitioners and schools under a shared philosophy.
Honor diversity while maintaining harmony and purpose.
